Practice Basic Strategy and Card Counting

1

STRATEGY

Practice basic strategy without worrying about the coutcome. Try to understand the reasoning behind this strategy as you improve.

Your goal should be 100% success here; this part of blackjack is more memory than skill. Note that the house still has a slight edge against a "perfect" player who isn't counting*.

2

COUNT

Card counting is a form of advantage play that is powerful enough to give you a long-term edge in the game.

The purpose of this section is to practice counting cards at your own pace using the Hi-Lo system.

3

PLAY

Put it all together in a real blackjack simulation. Hone your skills on your own before heading to Vegas, or practice with friends using our multiplayer mode!

Generally, you want to bet more when the count is high, while maintaining basic strategy. Learn advanced techniques to maximize your advantage as you improve.
